What is This All About?

AgriFarm is your everyday partner in smart agriculture. Whether you're planting, irrigating, tracking sustainability, or just checking weather alerts — our platform helps you do it better. With powerful tools and a farmer-friendly design, AgriFarm brings technology directly to your fields.

What is AgriFarm?

AgriFarm is a farmer-focused digital tool that helps improve productivity and sustainability with smart technology:

🌾 Crop Planner
Predict yield, plan layouts, and get companion suggestions.
💧 Water Simulator
Simulate water waste & get efficiency tips.
📈 Dashboard
Track EcoScore, water saved, and soil health.
📄 Farm Reports
Export PDF reports weekly/monthly.
⛅ Weather Alerts
Get local drought/flood alerts & smart tips.
🔐 Blockchain Storage
All data securely stored for full transparency.

How Farmer Dhruv Uses AgriFarm

Here’s a simple example of how AgriFarm helps real farmers every day:

  • Dhruv enters crop: Corn, soil: Sandy, area: 2 acres
  • AgriFarm predicts yield and shows companion crops like Beans
  • He simulates sprinkler waste and switches to drip irrigation
  • A rain alert shows – he adds mulch and saves water
  • Downloads a PDF report of yield, water use, and progress
  • All data is saved forever using blockchain
